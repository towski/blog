i love potential or “go to the small show that no one goes to”

October 13, 2007

one universal fact is unchangeable. I love potential.
This means: don’t go to any music event which more than 100 people go to. Don’t fall in love with the girl who is as beautiful as she’s ever going to be. Try something different. Risk your god given life.

I’m at a show right now, and the first band had an audience of 50 people. They were fucking amazing. Blew my mind. And their record probably isn’t half as good. But who cares. This next band has an audience of roughly 200. They’re shite.

Same for women. Potential is what drives anyone crazy. The potential to drive 3000 miles together after robbing a bank. The potential to change absolutely everything in your life just to laugh madly at one small thing. If you can’t talk to me about potential, possibility, and have an imagination, I just get bored. And I know I’m not happy and I’m alone again. But I can’t sit still. I can’t sit around while nothing happens. I don’t want to have sex with the blonde bombshell. I want to laugh insanely with the depressive new find. It plagues me to no end. Maybe one day ill settle down and marry the best possible candidate and cart around a stroller. But until then, anything is possible, including nothing. Shrug.
